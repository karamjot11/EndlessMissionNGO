About

Empowering change with Endless Mission

Endless Mission, Founded in May 2021, is working relentlessly with the motto of "Sarbat Da Bhalla" (welfare of all). We are a team of young and energetic people who want to bring a change in society by bringing a sustainable impact in the fields of environment protection, education and healthcare. We have more than 1000 volunteers across India working tirelessly for the people.

Found in May 2021, We are a team of young and energetic people who want to bring about a change in the society by bringing a sustainable impact in the fields of education and healthcare.

Apart from this we have been working on procuring medicines which are required to covid patients and have been giving them for free to needy people. We have also started distributing ration kits for jobless people and needful people. We have also been working on delivering amrit jal and kadah prashad of various gurudwaras at the doorsteps.

We believe in WELFARE OF ALL which is also our motto which encourage us to serve people even more efficiently.

About

Our Aim

To ensure welfare, provide aid to poverty and disease struck poor people or in respect to social groups such as the elderly or children, provide educational sources to poor etc.

Our Vision

Building a world where all communities have full access to innovative, high-quality, affordable, evidence-based sexual and reproductive health services, educational resources, food sources and basic necessities.

Our Mission

To prevent and end homelessness for families with children in the communities we serve through housing, resources and opportunity.
