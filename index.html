About - Endless Mission

Our Aim

To ensure welfare, provide aid to poverty and disease struck poor people or in respect to social groups such as the elderly or children, provide educational sources to poor etc.

Our Vision

Building a world where all communities have full access to innovative, high-quality, affordable, evidence-based sexual and reproductive health services, educational resources, food sources and basic necessities.

Our Mission

To prevent and end homelessness for families with children in the communities we serve through housing, resources and opportunity.

Our Causes

Provide Medical Care and Services

The Covid-19 pandemic has claimed millions of lives (and counting) and strained healthcare systems around the world. While the pandemic’s worst days are likely behind us, it remains a persistent concern alongside mental health and other medical issues.

Assistance during Outbreaks and Pandemics

Provides all kinds of medical care, but during outbreaks and pandemics helps by supplying things like dry rations, hygiene kits, and education kits.

Addressing Hunger

Famine occurs when countries or regions get hit with extreme food scarcity. Causes include wars, chronic poverty, natural disasters, and climate change. Getting enough food is essential to every person’s life.
